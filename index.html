LR-JET Weight & Balance

LR-JET Weight & Balance is no longer.

I started working on a new and improved version of this application some time ago. Back then, the pace of change in the Apple developer world was a lot slower. These days, things are happening a lot more quickly. It's difficult to keep up if you're not doing this full-time. Back then, I was working a full-time job that afforded me a lot of time to work on side projects. These days, my full-time job doesn't let me do a lot of sitting - at least not sitting in a place where I can work on projects like this. There just aren't enough hours in the day to develop and maintain an application like this and attend to the other areas of my life. So sadly, it's time to close out this chapter.

Thank you to all of you who have used this application at one time or another.
